🍋 How to make lemonade 🍋

🍋 Total prep and cook time 15 min. 6-8 servings Ingriediants 6-8 lemons 6 cups water 1 1/2 cups of sugar 1 1/2 cups of boiling water Optional: fresh mint leaves for garnishing Ice cubes 🍋

🍋 First start boiling the water.Put your sugar in a cup . Then put your boiling water on top of it. Stir the sugar water until the sugar dissolves. Set aside 🍋

🍋 Cut the lemons in halves then juice the lemons in a juicer to make 1 1/2 cups of lemon juice. Remember to strain out seeds and pulp. Set aside 🍋

🍋 Put 6 cups of regular water in cup. set aside 🍋

🍋 You get the sugar water, the lemon juice, and the water together. Pour all of it together. Mix really good. 🍋

🍋 After you mixed you can put Ice cubes to make it colder and refreshing. Also to garnish it you can put fresh mint leaves. Then drink and enjoy. 🍋
